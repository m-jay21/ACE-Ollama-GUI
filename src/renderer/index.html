What's Possible

  • • LoRA fine-tuning on 7B parameter models
  • • Small datasets (100-1000 examples)
  • • Domain adaptation for specific use cases
  • • Compact storage (~100MB adapter files)
  • • Consumer hardware (8GB+ RAM)

Current Limitations

  • • No full fine-tuning (requires 40GB+ VRAM)
  • • Limited to 7B models (no 13B+ models)
  • • Training time (2-8 hours typical)
  • • Data quality dependent (garbage in = garbage out)
  • • No real-time training (batch process only)
